Building Customer-Facing Dashboards for SaaS Products
A practical, hands-on course for teams looking to add real-time dashboards to their SaaS applications.
This workshop is intended for developers of SaaS applications looking to take real-time user data and build customer-facing dashboards. Whether you're a cloud service like Vercel, a creator service like Canva, or a startup like Dub.co, your customers will love being able to see their usage stats for your product in real-time.
