total_members
Usage:
{{total_members}}
The total_members helper outputs a rounded number of total members from your Ghost publication in a human readable format. Example:
{{
total_members
}}
If you have 1225 members, it will output
1,200+
.
For values above 100,000 it will output
100k+
and
3m+
respectively.
