Should you build or buy Usage-based Pricing?
Developers at API-first companies have a question to answer: Should we build usage-based pricing on our own or buy a SaaS tool off the shelf? Join us as we discuss the pros and cons of each approach with Alex Dovenmuehle, a usage-based pricing expert who has built UBP systems for both Heroku and Mattermost.
In this livestream, we'll help you answer the build vs. buy question for usage-based pricing (UBP) by answering the following questions:
What is the SaaS landscape for usage-based pricing? Who are the players to consider and what do they offer?
What is the basic architecture of a usage-based pricing system?
Why should you build UBP on your own?
Why should you
not
build UBP on your own?
How should you approach the question of build vs. buy for your own application?
